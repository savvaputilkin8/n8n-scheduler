Limitations & Notes
  • Background sync requires installing this app to your home screen (Android/Chrome). Without it, the tab must remain open for scheduled triggers.
  • iOS requires the app to be added to the home screen (iOS 16.4+) for background sync. Background sync may still be limited.
  • Minimum interval for background sync in Chrome is ~1 hour. For sub-hourly triggers, keep the tab open.
  • n8n form embed may fail if your n8n instance has restrictive X-Frame-Options headers.
